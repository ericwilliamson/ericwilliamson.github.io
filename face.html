Face Detection & Recognition

Based off of Willow Garage's OpenCV v2.0, I have created a 80-90% accurate face recognition program based off of one training image. The program is accurate with a dataset of up to 10,000 images. After that, the accuracy degrades.

To achieve such high accuracy off of just one training image (industry standards usually need 10 images to get near the same accuracy), I first apply a combination of light normalizing processes to the image. After that, I detect the eyes in the image and rotate it so that the face is perfectly horizontal. From there, I can determine the gender and ethnicity of the subject, as well as general age range. With this information, the dataset of 10,000 images can be greatly reduced to just a couple hundred faces. This entire process takes less than 100 milliseconds.
